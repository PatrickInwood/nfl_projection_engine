🏈 NFL Projection Engine

A fantasy football research hub powered by live Sleeper data and Claude AI — built by Patrick Inwood.

Features
📊

Rankings

Weekly player projections ranked by projected fantasy points. Filter by position (QB, RB, WR, TE, FLEX, K, D/ST), toggle PPR / Half PPR / Standard scoring, and hide players on bye week.

🛡

D/ST Rankings

Defensive unit rankings with fully customizable scoring. Adjust points for sacks, touchdowns, interceptions, fumbles, and safety values to match your league's exact settings.

🔍

Player Search

Search any active NFL player and instantly view their current week projection, injury status, team, position, and upcoming matchup — all pulled live from Sleeper.

🗂

Roster Builder

Enter your full roster — starters and bench — and let the optimizer build your ideal starting lineup. Supports custom slot configurations including Triple Flex leagues.

⚖️

Start / Sit

Compare any two players head-to-head. Claude analyzes their projections, matchups, injury status, and recent form to give you a clear start recommendation with reasoning.

🔄

Trade Analyzer

Build out both sides of any trade and get a Claude-powered evaluation. Factors in projected value, positional scarcity, roster construction, and rest-of-season outlook.

📋

Waiver Wire

Add players you're targeting off waivers and get Claude's ranked pickup recommendations. Includes live Trending Adds showing the most-added players on Sleeper in the last 24 hours.

🚑

Injury Report

Live injury designations for all active NFL players this week. Statuses sourced directly from Sleeper's player database and updated throughout the week as reports come in.

📅

Bye Week Calendar

Full 18-week bye calendar for all 32 NFL teams, organized by division. Auto-updates to the current season's schedule. See at a glance which weeks your players are unavailable.

📈

Game Log

Historical box scores for any NFL player with game-by-game stats, opponent, home/away, and actual weather conditions. Position-specific columns for QB, RB, WR, TE, and K.
